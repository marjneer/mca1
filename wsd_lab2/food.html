Momo is the most popular snack in Delhi. They are served with spicy red chutney which compliments the stuffing of the momo really well.

Paranthe

Paranthe, or stuffed flatbreads, is a beloved and iconic part of Delhi's culinary heritage. Paranthe Wali Gali, a narrow lane in the heart of Chandni Chowk, is renowned for its variety of stuffed paranthas and has become synonymous with this delectable treat.

Chaat

Renowned across India and beyond, these savoury snacks, including golgappe, aloo tikki, papdi chaat, dahi bhalla, and more, tantalize taste buds with their explosion of sweet, spicy, and tangy tastes.
